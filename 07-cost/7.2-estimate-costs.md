# 7.2 Estimate Costs

"The process of developing an approximation of the monetary resources needed to
complete project work." [[1]](../home.md#references)

- Define the cost unit
- Define the method to calculate the estimated cost

## Definitions

- Cost Unit: Dollar (US$)
- cO: Optimistic cost
- cM: Medium cost
- cP: Pessimistic cost
- cE: Estimated cost

```math
cE = (cO + 4 * cM + cE) / 6
```
