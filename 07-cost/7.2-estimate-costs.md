## Definitions

- Define the cost unit
- Define the method to calculate the estimated cost

- cO: Optimistic cost
- cM: Medium cost
- cP: Pessimistic cost
- cE: Estimated cost

- Cost Unit: Dollar (US$)

```math
cE = (cO + 4 * cM + cE) / 6
```
